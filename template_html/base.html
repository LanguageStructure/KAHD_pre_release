Additional Information

The Arawa Lexical Database (ALeD) is the first part of the Arawa Languages Resources Project (ArLaR) to be made public. Its goal is to provide data (lexical, moprhological, and syntactic) for linguistic analysis. We invite and welcome suggestions, criticisms, and collaborators.
